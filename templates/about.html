How It Works

Understanding our Machine Learning & NLP Pipeline

1. Text Preprocessing

Before the machine learning model can understand the news, we must clean the raw text.

  • Lowercasing: Uniform text case.
  • Removing Punctuation & URLs: Stripping noise.
  • Stopwords Removal: Removing common words (the, is, in).
  • Stemming: Reducing words to their root form (e.g., "running" to "run").

Raw Text ➔ Clean Text

2. Feature Extraction (TF-IDF)

Computers don't understand words; they understand numbers. We use Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency to convert text into mathematical vectors.

TF-IDF measures how important a word is to a document in a collection or corpus. Words that appear frequently in one document but rarely in others are given higher weight.

Words ➔ Vectors [0.1, 0.5, 0.0, ...]

3. Logistic Regression

The core intelligence of TruthAI. Logistic Regression is a statistical model that estimates the probability of an event occurring.

In our case, it calculates the probability that a given vector of text belongs to the "Fake News" category vs the "Real News" category based on patterns learned from 50,000+ training articles.

Vectors ➔ Probability %
